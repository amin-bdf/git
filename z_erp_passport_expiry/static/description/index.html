Overview

"This Module will helps to manage the passport information of the employee and the expiry notification of the passport based on the expiry date and notification days configurations..!"

Using this module, to manage the passport information of the employee and the expiry notification of the passport based on the expiry date and notification days configurations..!

From the Settings --> General Settings -->Employees we can configure the notification to be received before the expiry date..!

In Employee Master, we can fill the all the information about passport as shown..!

If any employee's Passport will expire with the configured days means they will get a notification.!
