Overview

"This Module will helps to manage the Visa information of the employee and the expiry notification of the Visa based on the expiry date and notification days configurations..!"

Using this module, to manage the Visa information of the employee and the expiry notification of the Visa based on the expiry date and notification days configurations..!

From the Settings --> General Settings -->Employees we can configure the notification to be received before the expiry date..!

In Employee Master, we can fill the all the information about Visa as shown..!

If any employee's Visa will expire with the configured days means they will get a notification.!
